Powerful Features

Interactive Crime Map

Visualize crime hotspots and trends with our interactive map. See real-time reports and historical data to stay informed about safety in your area.

Real-time Reporting

Report crimes instantly with our user-friendly interface. Add location, description, and upload media to provide authorities with critical information.

Predictive Analytics
